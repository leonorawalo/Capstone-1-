Our Story

Building the future of sustainable urban transport

About Tukii

Founded in 2020, Tukii emerged from a simple observation: cities needed smarter, cleaner transportation that didn't sacrifice convenience or comfort. What started as a pilot program with 10 electric tuktuks has grown into a network spanning 15 cities and serving over 50,000 daily riders.

We're not just a transport serviceâ€”we're a movement toward sustainable urban living. Every ride with Tukii represents a choice for cleaner air, reduced congestion, and a more livable city.

Our Mission

To revolutionize urban mobility by providing accessible, sustainable, and delightful transportation experiences that benefit riders, drivers, and communities alike.

Our Vision

A world where clean, efficient public transport is the preferred choice, creating cities that are healthier, more connected, and more enjoyable for everyone.

What Sets Us Apart

Electric-First

Our entire fleet runs on clean electric power, eliminating emissions and reducing noise pollution in urban centers.

Driver Partnership

We believe in fair compensation and professional development. Our drivers are partners, not contractors, with comprehensive benefits and training.

Technology Integration

Smart routing algorithms, real-time tracking, and predictive demand modeling ensure efficient service and minimal wait times.

Community Focus

We invest in the communities we serve through local hiring, partnerships with small businesses, and support for urban development initiatives.

Safety Standards

Rigorous vehicle maintenance, comprehensive driver screening, and continuous safety monitoring keep our riders secure.

Transparent Pricing

No surge pricing, no hidden fees. What you see is what you pay, with discounts for regular riders.

Our Impact

Since our launch, Tukii has prevented over 2,500 tons of CO2 emissions, equivalent to planting 40,000 trees. We've created 500+ jobs in the communities we serve and have become the preferred transport choice for thousands of daily commuters.

But we're just getting started. Our expansion plans include new cities, innovative vehicle designs, and partnerships that will make sustainable transport accessible to even more people.

Leadership Team

Tukii is led by a diverse team of transportation innovators, technologists, and sustainability advocates committed to transforming how cities move.
