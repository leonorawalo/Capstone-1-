For Best Availability

Cancellation Policy

Free Cancellation: Cancel up to 15 minutes before scheduled pickup time at no charge.

Late Cancellation: Cancellations within 15 minutes of pickup incur a $4 fee to compensate the driver.

No-Show: Failure to meet driver results in full fare charge. Please cancel if plans change.

Driver Cancellation: If a driver cancels, you're immediately matched with another driver at no additional cost, or receive a full refund.
