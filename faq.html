Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about riding with Tukii

Booking & Rides

How do I book a ride?

You can book through our mobile app, website at tukii.com/book, or by calling +1-800-TUKII-GO. The app offers the fastest booking experience with instant confirmation and real-time tracking.

How far in advance can I schedule a ride?

You can schedule rides up to 7 days in advance. For airport transfers and special events, we recommend booking at least 24 hours ahead to ensure availability.

What's the maximum passenger capacity?

Standard Tukii vehicles seat 3 passengers comfortably. For groups of 4-6, we can arrange multiple vehicles to travel together. Contact us for larger group bookings.

Can I bring luggage?

Yes! Each vehicle can accommodate 2-3 standard suitcases or equivalent luggage. For oversized items or extra baggage, please mention this when booking so we can assign an appropriate vehicle.

How long will my driver wait?

Drivers wait 3 minutes free of charge for standard pickups. Airport transfers include 60 minutes of free wait time from flight landing. Additional wait time is charged at $0.40 per minute.

Pricing & Payment

How is the fare calculated?

Fares include a base fee of $4.50 covering the first 2 km, plus $1.80 per additional kilometer. Wait time over 3 minutes is $0.40 per minute. All prices include taxes, fees, and gratuity.

Do you have surge pricing?

No. We believe in transparent, consistent pricing. Our rates remain the same during peak hours, bad weather, and special events.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, cash, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and other mobile payment apps. Corporate accounts can opt for monthly invoicing.

Do I need to tip the driver?

Tips are included in all fares, so additional tipping is not expected. However, if you'd like to show extra appreciation for exceptional service, drivers can accept tips through the app or in cash.

Can I get a receipt?

Yes! Digital receipts are automatically sent to your email after each ride. You can also view and download all receipts from your account history.

Safety & Comfort

Are your vehicles safe in bad weather?

Yes. All Tukii vehicles feature weather protection including enclosed cabins, side curtains, and climate control. During severe weather, we may temporarily suspend service for safety.

Do you provide child seats?

Yes, complimentary child seats are available for infants and toddlers. Request one when booking and specify your child's age and weight for proper seat assignment.

Are pets allowed?

Small pets in carriers are welcome at no extra charge. Medium to large pets require advance notice. Service animals are always welcome and never require prior approval.

How are drivers vetted?

All drivers undergo comprehensive background checks, have minimum 5 years professional driving experience, complete safety training, and maintain clean driving records with regular monitoring.

What if I don't feel safe during a ride?

Use the in-app emergency button or call +1-800-TUKII-911 immediately. Our 24/7 safety team will assist you and can dispatch emergency services if needed.

Technical & Account

Do I need an account to book?

No, you can book as a guest through our website or phone. However, creating an account enables faster bookings, ride history, saved locations, and exclusive member benefits.

Can I request a specific driver?

Yes! If you've had a great experience with a particular driver, you can add them as a favorite and request them for future rides, subject to availability.

How do I track my ride?

Real-time GPS tracking is available in the app from the moment your booking is confirmed until ride completion. You can also share your live location with friends or family.

What if I left something in the vehicle?

Contact our lost and found team immediately at +1-800-TUKII-LF or through the app. We maintain all found items for 30 days and will coordinate return with you.

How do I report an issue?

Use the "Report Issue" feature in the app, email support@tukii.com, or call our customer service line. All reports are reviewed within 24 hours and taken seriously.

Special Services

Do you offer accessible transportation?

Yes, we have vehicles equipped for passengers with mobility needs, including ramp access. Request accessible transport when booking to ensure proper vehicle assignment.

Can I book for someone else?

Absolutely! You can book and pay for rides for family members, friends, or colleagues. Just enter their pickup details and contact information when booking.

Do you offer corporate accounts?

Yes! Corporate accounts include monthly billing, detailed expense reporting, priority booking, and volume discounts. Contact business@tukii.com for more information.

Are city tours customizable?

Yes! While we offer standard tour routes, you can customize your tour to include specific locations or themes. Discuss preferences with your driver or mention them when booking.

Still have questions?

Our support team is here to help. Reach out via email, phone, or visit our contact page for more ways to get in touch.
