Safety Tips for Riders

Before Your Ride

  • Verify driver photo and license plate match your booking
  • Check driver's rating and profile before entering
  • Share trip details with a trusted contact

During Your Ride

  • Always wear your seatbelt
  • Follow the route on the app
  • Keep personal belongings secure
  • Report any concerns immediately

After Your Ride

  • Rate your experience honestly
  • Report any issues within 24 hours
  • Check for personal items before exiting
  • Contact lost and found if needed

Reporting & Response

We take every safety report seriously and investigate all incidents thoroughly. Our dedicated safety team reviews complaints within 24 hours and takes appropriate action, which may include additional driver training, suspension, or permanent removal from the platform.

Your feedback helps us maintain the highest safety standards. All reports are confidential, and we protect whistleblower identities.
