Point-to-Point Rides

Our core service delivers you directly from pickup to destination with no detours. Perfect for daily commutes, meetings, or getting around town efficiently.

  • Real-time GPS tracking
  • Climate-controlled vehicles
  • Upfront pricing with no surprises

Hourly Rentals

Need flexibility? Book a Tukii by the hour and have your personal driver navigate the city while you focus on your tasks. Ideal for running multiple errands or business appointments.

  • Flexible routing and stops
  • 2, 4, or 8-hour packages available
  • Priority rebooking for regular customers
